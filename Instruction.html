Instruction for Membership Application of DCCI

Eligibility of Membership

Persons, firms, companies (including multinational companies) and corporate bodies (including sector corporations, banks, insurance etc.) engaged in trade, industry or any other kind of business are eligible for membership of the Dhaka Chamber of Commerce & Industry (DCCI).

FOREIGN COMPANIES: Foreign Companies, Corporate Bodies operating in Bangladesh shall also be eligible for membership of the Dhaka Chamber of Commerce & Industry.

Application Procedures:  Every candidate for membership shall submit the prescribed online application from through the One Stop Service System. The DCCI authority holds the right to admit or decline a candidate for admission as a member as they may think fit.

Sample Copy of Online Application Form

Required Documents

    1. Scanned copy of your company/organization’s trade license.
    2. Memorandum and Articles of Association (MOA & AOA) for Company
    3. Copy of your organization’s tax certificate
    4. Copy of National ID or passport of the authorized representative(s)

Application Fee:

A service charge of Tk 250 has to be paid for submitting any application through one stop service system. Make this payment through online payment gateway. Then pay the prescribed fee while filling the application form of DCCI.

Application Submission

Carefully fill out the membership application form with accurate and up-to-date information.

Attach all the required documents to the application form. Double-check that all the documents are complete and legible.

Review and Verification:

Submit your completed application form along with the supporting documents to the DCCI office.

The DCCI staff will review your application and documents for completeness and accuracy.

They may contact you if any additional information or clarification is required.

Approval Process

Once your application is reviewed and verified, it will be presented to the DCCI's Membership Committee for approval. The Membership Committee will assess your eligibility and make a decision based on the provided information. The decision will be communicated to you through the contact details provided in the application form.

Membership Fee

If your membership application is approved, you will be required to pay the applicable membership fee within the specified timeframe. The DCCI will provide you with detailed instructions regarding the payment process.

Membership Certificate

Upon receipt of the membership fee, the DCCI will issue your official membership certificate.

You can collect the certificate from the OSS System.

For any further inquiries or assistance regarding the membership application process, feel free to contact the Dhaka Chamber of Commerce & Industry directly through their official contact channels.
